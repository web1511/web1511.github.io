SHIRLEY ZHANG

张雪莉

著名珐琅珠宝设计师

Shirley Zhang的设计灵感大多源自于她对生活哲学的独到感悟。“永恒”与“生命”是她在创作道路上始终追求的主题，一方面她能够将转瞬即逝的事物以珠宝语言的固态形式和收藏属性呈现出来；另一方面，她探讨内心与精神的长存并借宝石之美传承历史文化的深度以及与佩戴者的内心呼应。
Gifted with a natural talent for colour coordination and an adept at craftsmanship, Shirley Zhang, founded the high-end jewelry brand in her own name in 1994. Now “Shirley Zhang Bespoke Jewel Boutique” has flourished in more than 14 major Chinese cities, including Beijing and Shanghai. Her expert technique, combined with exquisite colour compositions, result in the most beautiful expressions of artistic charm and character.

MARIE PAULE QUERCY

Marie Paule Quercy

法国顶尖珠宝设计师

Marie Paule出生于盛产艺术家的巴黎。早期特别钟情于绘画，并在青年时期提前2年修完UCAD专业设计课程。凭借其优异的学识，她的作品很快得到了各个珠宝豪门的赏识。并于1998年开始先后在Hermes、Tiffany、Graff、VanCleef&Arples等顶级珠宝品牌担任设计师角色。
Born in Paris, Marie-Paule Quercy grew up in Montparnasse, the capital leading artistic neighborhood, which encouraged her to expand her talent for drawing. Once admitted to UCAD (School of Decorative Arts) two years in advance. With the professional of jewelry, she got a lot of admiration from different. Since 1998, she worked for Hermes, Tiffany, Graff, VanCleef & Areples Fred as designes.

MARIE PIERRE CANIVET

Marie Pierre Canivet

法国自由高端珠宝设计

Marie-Pierre,法国自由设计师，承担高端珠宝设计。1983-1987四年在CHAUMET的的经验，Marie-Pierre的设计能力得到业界认可。1988年开始，她先后在梵克雅宝、宝诗龙、ASPREY-BOIVIN、MIKIMOTO、CHATILA担任设计师，在巴塞尔珠宝展上为客户定制高端珠宝首饰、日内瓦高定钟表，并于2000年荣获大溪地珍珠奖杯。
Marie Pierre, freelance and contractor to high-end jewelers. From 1983 to 1987, she became Paris in house designer junior designer there after full-time designer. Since 1988, she have worked for Van Cleef & Appels, Boucheron, ASPREY-BOIVIN, MIKIMOTO and CHATILA. She was also awarded the internet jury selection of best brooch in TAHITIAN PEARL TROPHY 2000, designed the fine jewelry for customers in Basel Fair, and Geneve.

CIGA LONG

龙梓嘉

明星御用珠宝设计师

龙梓嘉，万法一堂艺术品有限公司的董事、Ciga Long 个人设计品牌的创始人、明星御用珠宝设计师（为刘涛、李湘、李小璐等订制珠宝）。 曾就读于中国美术学院及意大利佛罗伦萨珠宝设计学院。龙梓嘉出生在曲艺世家，中国古典艺术的熏陶奠定了他复古风格珠宝设计的基石。
Ciga Long, the founder and designer of Wan Fa Yi Tang. His collections have been followed by many TV and movie stars, Liu Tao, Li Xiaolu, Li Xiang etc. Born in folk art family, influenced by Chinese classical art, learned in China Academy of Art and Le Arti Orafe Jewelry School & Academy, his retro style of jewelry design being unique.

XU KE

徐可

中国地质大学珠宝学院讲师

中国地质大学珠宝学院讲师。毕业于湖北美术学院油画专业，设计艺术学硕士。从事首饰设计及首饰传统工艺花丝工艺等方面的研究和教学。凭借对于传统首饰设计工艺的专研，在传统与现代结合的设计中有着独树一帜的风格。
Xu Ke, Lecturer of jewelry academy, China University of Geosciences, Graduated from Hubei Institute of Fine Arts, majoring in oil painting, master of art in design. Engaged in teaching and research in jewelry design and jewelry traditional craft filigree craft. By virtue of the traditional jewelry design technology of the special research, Xu Ke always holding a unique style.

关于赫美

About Hemei

赫美珠宝由上市公司赫美集团（股票代码：002356）全资控股，成立于2016年。是集零售服务、珠宝加工、人才管理和设计师培养的珠宝创新消费平台，并将成为消费者选择个性化珠宝饰品的主要消费平台和渠道。赫美珠宝旗下公司包括：艺术设计中心、Artoriz、精工坊、艺术设计学院。
设计艺术中心，以“不做设计，只孕育生命”为信念，致力于打造时尚与艺术的跨界合作。Artoriz, 作为赫美旗下优质的珠宝买手平台，集合了独立设计师、跨界艺术家、顶级工匠，以买手式经营模式打造实体珠宝概念店。精工坊，拥有全国最为突出的手绘珐琅工艺工匠，汇集了数十位资历深厚的珠宝加工匠人。艺术设计学院，结合工艺培训、艺术研究于现代设计，创造一个全新的学习氛围。

HEMEI JEWELRY,as wholly-owned subsidiary of Shenzhen Hemei Group Co., LTD (stock cod: 002356) was founded in 2016. HEMEI JEWELRY strives to provide an innovative platform of jewelry in combining retail, processing, talent management and cultivation in order to offer a notable venue for our customers to pick and choose individualized jewelries and accessories. With sub-branches H-Art Design Center, Hormone, Top-Atelier and H-College.
H-Art Design Center committed in building up the cross-border cooperation of fashion and art with faith of “To nurture is our design”. Artoriz, an quality jewelry buyer platform, gather independent designers, artists, experts in craftsmanship and technical skills. Concept store with buyer-based operation. Top-Atelier, home to some of the most exceptional enameling experts and more than a dozen experienced jewelers.
H-College incorporate the training of technical skills, artistic studies and researches and contemporary design to create a lively learning environment.
